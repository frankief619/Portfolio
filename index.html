About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Frank Flannery, an IT professional with a passion for leveraging technology to create meaningful and efficient solutions. With a diverse background in business analysis, project management, and UX/UI design, I bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and technical expertise to every project.

Whether it's improving user experience or optimizing processes, I am dedicated to delivering results that exceed expectations.

My Process

From Concept to Completion

Requirements Gathering/User Research

Understanding project goals and user needs is the foundation of any design. I engage with stakeholders to identify key requirements, ensuring a clear vision for the project.

Wireframing

Wireframing helps visualize the structure and layout of the product. It’s a crucial step where ideas take form, enabling early feedback before the design process begins.

Prototyping

Prototyping brings the wireframes to life. I create interactive prototypes that allow stakeholders to experience the design’s functionality and flow before development.

User Testing

Testing the design with real users helps identify usability issues and areas for improvement. I iterate on feedback to ensure the product is intuitive and user-friendly.

Validating

Validation involves checking if the design meets user needs and business objectives. I use data and feedback to make informed decisions, refining the design where necessary.

Accessibility

Accessibility is integral to the design process. I ensure that the product is usable by people of all abilities, complying with accessibility standards for a more inclusive experience.
